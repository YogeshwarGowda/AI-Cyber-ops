Our ISO 27001 Service Offering

Achieve internationally recognised information security compliance with guidance from certified ISO 27001:2022 Lead Auditors. We assist organisations in designing and implementing their Information Security Management System (ISMS), conducting internal audits, identifying gaps, and preparing thoroughly for successful certification.

Our ISO 27001 Services Include

  • Comprehensive ISO 27001 Gap Assessment and readiness evaluation
  • Development of complete ISMS documentation, including policies, procedures, and SOPs
  • Risk assessment and risk treatment planning in accordance with ISO 27005
  • Creation and maintenance of an asset register and Statement of Applicability (SoA)
  • Expert guidance for implementing required security controls
  • Internal audits carried out by certified ISO 27001 Lead Auditors
  • Support with corrective actions and audit evidence preparation
  • Full preparation for Stage 1 and Stage 2 certification audits
  • Assistance in choosing a suitable UKAS-accredited certification body

Our Implementation Approach

  1. Assessment & Scoping – We begin by analysing your business processes and current security posture to define the scope of your ISMS.
  2. Gap Analysis – We evaluate existing practices to identify control gaps, weaknesses, and improvement areas.
  3. Documentation Development – We prepare the required ISMS documentation, including policies, procedures, and operational controls.
  4. Controls Implementation – We guide your team in deploying the technical and organisational measures needed to meet ISO 27001 requirements.
  5. Internal Audit – Certified ISO 27001 Lead Auditors perform internal audits to verify compliance and identify corrective actions.
  6. Certification Preparation – We assist with readiness for Stage 1 and Stage 2 external audits, ensuring your organisation is fully prepared for certification.
