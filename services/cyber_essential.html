Cyber Essentials Certification

Cyber Essentials

Cyber Essentials is a government-backed certification scheme that helps organisations protect themselves against the most common cyber threats. It works through a guided self-assessment process verified by an independent assessor.

Participants answer a standard set of questions covering core security areas: user access control, secure configuration, software updates, firewalls, and malware protection.

Once your assessment is reviewed and approved, you will receive your official Cyber Essentials certificate and can display the badge on your website and communications.
