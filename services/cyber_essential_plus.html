Cyber Essentials Plus

Comprehensive Technical Assurance

Cyber Essentials Plus is a technical audit of your IT systems, building upon the verified self-assessment of Cyber Essentials, which is a prerequisite to achieving this level.

Although based on the same five technical controls, Cyber Essentials Plus goes further by conducting a hands-on verification to ensure that the controls are properly implemented.

The audit covers:

  • A representative set of user devices
  • All internet gateways
  • All servers with internet-facing services

This certification provides a higher level of assurance that your organization is truly compliant with the NCSC’s cybersecurity standards.

Pricing: Cyber Essentials Plus is priced according to the size and complexity of your network.
